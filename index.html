The story

The characters within this novel consist of a high school professor, Marty Dirjke, who is forced to overcome an old adversary on his playing field the world of espionage. The professor is flanked by three youngsters. One is his nephew, Danny, who is in New York scouting colleges to pursue as a student athlete and for a scholarship. Barry is a know-it-all former student of the professor that is as neurotic as a young man can be in historical settings. Lastly, Ana is a fierce young woman who has been a friend of Barrys while growing up in New York City. As a native New Yorker, she refuses to back down from a threat to her home Manhattan..
